# Papers

One page per primary-reading paper, grouped by course section. Each page is a
reading companion (Why assigned, Reading guidance, two-tier Background,
Motivating questions, Supplementary), not a summary of the paper's findings.

Pages are added with `/generate-paper-summary`. When you add a paper, list it
here under its section heading as `[author-year-shortname](author-year-shortname.md) — short descriptor`.
